## Academic and Scientific Research

Tools for literature discovery, summarization, citation analysis, and reference
management in academic and scientific work.

> [!CAUTION]
> AI tools can speed up a research workflow, but they are not a replacement for
> human judgment, critical thinking, and rigorous methodology. Always evaluate
> results critically and account for potential biases, limitations, and
> uncertainties when interpreting AI-generated output.

## Deep Research Tools

Deep research tools are AI agents that autonomously search and read many online
sources, then reason over the material to produce structured, cited reports.

> [!CAUTION]
> None of these tools are perfect, and they still require human oversight to
> ensure accuracy, address potential biases, and critically evaluate the
> generated information.

| Tool             | Description                                                                                                                 | License     | Pricing     |
|:-----------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|:-----------:|:-----------:|
| [Claude Research](https://www.anthropic.com/news/research) | Anthropic's research mode in Claude, which runs multi-step searches across the web and connected sources to produce cited reports. | [<img src="../Images/Licence/proprietary.svg" alt="proprietary" width="90" height="15" />](https://www.heavybit.com/library/article/open-source-vs-proprietary) | [<img src="../Images/Pricing/Freemium.svg" alt="Freemium" width="80" height="20" />](https://builtin.com/articles/freemium)  |
